About Sleep Better Daily

Sleep Better Daily is a research-driven publication focused on the physiology of sleep and recovery. Our coverage centers on the mechanisms behind sleep onset, deep sleep architecture, cortisol regulation, and the nutritional factors, particularly magnesium, that influence nervous system activity and GABA receptor function during the body's wind-down process.

Each article is structured around current sleep science research and is reviewed for accuracy before publication. We do not publish anecdotal wellness trends without grounding them in physiological mechanism. If a claim is made about a supplement, mineral, or sleep practice, the underlying biological rationale is explained alongside it.

Content focus areas: sleep onset latency, circadian rhythm regulation, magnesium and nervous system function, cortisol and stress-sleep interaction, and recovery physiology. Sources are drawn from peer-reviewed sleep research and clinical nutrition literature.
